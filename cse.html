About

The Depart­ment of Com­puter Sci­ence & Engi­neer­ing is estab­lished in the year 1996 and is incharged by Sri. K. Kishore Kumar, Assis­tant Pro­fes­sor, hav­ing 10 years expe­ri­ence in the field of Com­puter Sci­ence and Engi­neer­ing. The depart­ment has a team of well expe­ri­enced and well qual­i­fied fac­ulty. The depart­ment has estab­lished the fol­low­ing lab­o­ra­to­ries for effec­tive lab­o­ra­tory instruc­tion.

Computer Science Engineering Laboratories

  • Pro­gram­ming Lab with 40 Lat­est Con­fig­u­ra­tion Sys­tems
  • Oper­at­ing sys­tems Lab with 30 Lat­est Con­fig­u­ra­tion Sys­tems
  • Net­works Lab with 40 Lat­est Con­fig­u­ra­tion Sys­tems
  • Advanced DBMS Lab with 40 Lat­est Con­fig­u­ra­tion Sys­tems
In addi­tion to these, the depart­ment has estab­lished an advanced project lab­o­ra­tory with 30 Mul­ti­me­dia sys­tems for the ben­e­fit of Final year stu­dents to carry out their project works & pre­pare for tech­ni­cal paper pre­sen­ta­tions. The Depart­ment has arranged round the clock inter­net access through V-​sat con­nec­tiv­ity, which can be accessed over the cam­pus — wide LAN.
