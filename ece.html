About

The Depart­ment of Elec­tron­ics and Com­mu­ni­ca­tion Engi­neer­ing is estab­lished in the year 2011. The depart­ment has a team of well expe­ri­enced and well qual­i­fied fac­ulty. The depart­ment is incharged by Dr. B Bik­shalu who has 10 years of expe­ri­ence. The labs of the depart­ment were well estab­lished with all the nec­es­sary equip­ment and instru­men­ta­tion needed for impart­ing effec­tive lab­o­ra­tory instruction.

Electronics and Communication Engineering Laboratories

  • Elec­tronic Devices & Cir­cuits Lab
  • Lin­ear Inte­grated Cir­cuits Lab
  • Pulse & Dig­i­tal Cir­cuits Lab
  • Ana­log Com­mu­ni­ca­tions Lab
  • Dig­i­tal Com­mu­ni­ca­tions Lab
  • Micro­proces­sor & Micro­con­trollers Lab
  • E-​CAD Lab
  • Dig­i­tal Sig­nal Pro­cess­ing Lab
  • Microwave Engi­neer­ing Lab
In addi­tion to these, the depart­ment has estab­lished an advanced project lab­o­ra­tory with 30 Mul­ti­me­dia sys­tems for the ben­e­fit of Final year stu­dents to carry out their project works & pre­pare for tech­ni­cal paper pre­sen­ta­tions. The Depart­ment has arranged round the clock inter­net access through V-​sat con­nec­tiv­ity, which can be accessed over the cam­pus — wide LAN.
